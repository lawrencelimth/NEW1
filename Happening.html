Calender

Rev. Winston Tan was serving with the Public Work Department as a Technical Officer for two years after he graduated from Singapore Polytechnic.

He graduated from three colleges namely the Ecclesia Bible College (Hong Kong), Far East Advance School of Theology (Philippines) and Trinity Theological College (Singapore) and finally he received the Doctor of Divinity from India.

He was the Vicar of Church of The True Light and had served in the Anglican Church for the past 30 years. Prior to serving at the Anglican Diocese, he was serving with the Assemblies of God denomination for 10 years.
